The same dish exists for every body.

Most recipe apps treat dietary needs as filters that remove dishes from the
world: go vegan and the Döner disappears, develop a nut allergy and Pad Thai is
gone. MorphCook inverts this. Every dish exists as fully-authored variants —
vegan Döner, gluten-free Alfredo, keto burger — and your profile decides which
variant of each dish you see. You keep the whole cookbook.

Set your dietary avoidances, calorie target, time budget and language once, and
every recipe reshapes itself to match. Switch a dish to another variant any time
you like.

Features:

* Every dish authored as multiple real variants, not filtered away
* A profile that morphs the whole cookbook to your needs
* Step-by-step cook mode
* Shopping lists and meal planning
* Bilingual: English and German
* Encrypted, exportable backups (AES-256-GCM) that stay on your device

Privacy by design:

* Fully offline — the app makes no network requests
* No accounts, no ads, no analytics, no tracking, no runtime AI
* Requests no sensitive permissions
* Nothing ever leaves your device unless you export a backup yourself

MorphCook is free and open source software.
